To Die For Alfredo Recipe

Description

A delightful recipe for a homemade fettucine alfredo, its so creamy and delicious its to die for!

Ingredients

  • pasta
  • butter
  • cream
  • seasonings
  • cheese

Steps

  1. Cook and drain the pasta
  2. melt the butter and cream together on the stove, season and stir in cheese
  3. Mtoss the pasta in the cheese sauce
