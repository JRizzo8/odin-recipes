Super Nachos Recipe

Description

A delightful recipe for a homemade nachos, youll love the fiesta of flavors in your mouth.

Ingredients

  • ground beef
  • green onions
  • chopped tomatos
  • refried beans
  • salsa
  • sour cream

Steps

  1. Cook and drain the onions and meat
  2. line a baking sheet with foil
  3. add chips and sprinkle cheese on
  4. bake for 30 secs to melt cheese
  5. add other topping, bake to desired doneness
